Mamá Imelda

Mamá Imelda was Miguel's great-grandmother, the matriarch of the Rivera family. Strong, independent, brave. After her husband, a musician, abandoned the family and disappeared, she vowed that her family would not touch music. Besides, she raised her great-grandmother Coco by learning how to make shoes and turned her family into a famous family of shoemakers.
