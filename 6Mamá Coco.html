Mamá Coco

Coco is Miguel's great-grandmother, a confused old man who is Mig's closest and most important family member. Her mother Imelda raised her father alone after he left home, but she never forgot her father, always thinking about him. "Coco" is the most important connection point between the past and the future, between the world of the dead and the world, between memory and reality.
