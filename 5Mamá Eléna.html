Mamá Eléna

Coco's daughter and Miguel's grandmother. She's the ultimate authority in the entire Rivera family, which equates music with abandonment. She loved her family so much, she would do anything to protect them. When provoked, she would wave her slippers. When it came to musically related family prohibitions, Grandma became a staunch defender of tradition.
