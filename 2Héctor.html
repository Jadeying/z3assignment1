Héctor

A down-and-out drifter in the Land of the Dead who wants to use Miguel's help to get to the world of the living. He promised to help Miguel find Ernesto de la Cruz, the god of song. In return, Miguel promised to return Héctor's picture to his family's altar. When Héctor is completely forgotten in the world, he will be turned into a golden powder, facing the real death.
