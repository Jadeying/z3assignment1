Ernesto de la Cruz

He was a legendary Mexican musician, Miguel's idol. The charming and charismatic musician was revered by music fans around the world before his unexpected death, and even more so in the world of the dead. Miguel thinks he could be the great-grandfather who abandoned his family and children to pursue his musical dreams.
